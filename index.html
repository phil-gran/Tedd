Tips for getting the U-bill sent to you immediately

  Convincing a homeowner to send you their electricity bill right away can be challenging, but with the right approach, you will increase your chances of success. Here are some tips:

Establish rapport:

  Take the time to get to know the homeowner and build a relationship with them. This can help to establish trust and make them more comfortable sharing their electricity bill with you.

Explain the purpose:

  Explain the purpose of the electricity bill and why it is necessary for the solar presentation. For example, you can explain that you need to understand their energy usage patterns in order to provide a more accurate estimate of the potential savings from a solar system.

Emphasize the benefits:

  Highlight the benefits of the solar system and explain how the electricity bill will help you to provide a more accurate estimate of those benefits. For example, you can explain that a detailed electricity bill will allow you to identify areas where energy savings can be maximized.

Offer a solution:

  Offer a solution to any concerns or objections the homeowner may have about sending their electricity bill. For example, you can offer to email the bill securely or to redact any personal information that they may not want to share.

Be persistent:

   Be persistent, but not pushy. If the homeowner is not comfortable sending their electricity bill right away, offer to schedule a follow-up call or meeting to discuss it further.

  By approaching the situation in a friendly and professional manner, and highlighting the benefits and addressing any concerns the homeowner may have, you will increase your chances of convincing them to send you their electricity bill right away

Steps to guide a homeowner through the process of sending their electricity bill:

  1. Ask the homeowner if they have access to their electricity bill online or if they have a physical copy of it.
  2. If they have access to the bill online, ask them to log in to their account and locate the latest bill.
  3. If they have a physical copy, ask them to grab it.
  4. Once they have found the bill, ask them to look for the kilowatt-hour (kWh) consumption for the last 12 months and the total bill amount.
  5. If they prefer to send the bill via text, ask them to take a clear photo of the bill and text it to apricotenergyusa@gmail.com.
  6. If they prefer to send the bill via email, ask them to scan and attach the bill and email it to apricotenergyusa@gmail.com or save the images of the bill or take screenshots of the online bill and email it to apricotenergyusa@gmail.com
  7. If they are unable or unwilling to send it to you, ask them to tell you their kWh consumption for the last 12 months and the exact bill amount over the phone.
  8. Thank the homeowner for their cooperation and inform them that you will be able to assist them better with this information.
